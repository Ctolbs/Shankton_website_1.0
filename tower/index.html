Getting here
20-min walk to Gigante village on shaded jungle trails. 35 min by boat from San Juan del Sur. Danny handles airport pickups from Managua.

Food
We provide salt, pepper, hot sauce, olive oil, cooking oil, and filtered spring water (5-gallon jugs, more on request). If you're self-catering, stop at Colonia in Rivas — about 40 minutes out — on the way in. Gigante's village shops are limited. Private chef on request.

On the bunk room: The top-floor bunk room has ceiling fans but no A/C. It's designed for kids — cooler at night with the breeze. The four main bedrooms are fully air-conditioned. Nicaragua has occasional power outages. WiFi is available and we keep connectivity reliable. We'd rather tell you upfront.

Common questions

What's the cancellation policy?
Full refund for cancellations made at least 14 days before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations 7–14 days before check-in. No refund within 7 days of check-in. All cancellations must be made in writing to contact@shankton.com.
What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is at 4:00 PM. Check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available depending on the schedule — ask Nemer in advance.
How do I get to Playa Gigante?
Fly into Managua (MGA). Danny handles airport pickups directly — he'll stop at Colonia in Rivas on the way so you arrive with groceries. The drive is about 3 hours. You can also take a shuttle from Managua to San Juan del Sur and then a 35-minute boat to Gigante. Ask us to arrange either option when you book.
Is the water safe to drink?
We provide filtered spring water in 5-gallon jugs. Tap water is not for drinking. More jugs available on request — we keep the kitchen stocked before arrival.
How reliable is the WiFi?
WiFi is available and we work to keep it reliable. Nicaragua has occasional power outages — the property has backup for the essentials. If you're planning to work remotely, let us know and we'll make sure everything is set.
Can I hire a private chef?
Yes. Nemer arranges private chefs who cook at the property. This is one of the most popular add-ons — the outdoor dining area at Tower is built for it. Book through Nemer directly after reserving.
Can I bring a pet?
Pets are not permitted at Shankton Tower.
Is there a minimum stay?
Minimum stay is 1 night. For high season (November–March) we recommend at least 4–5 nights to make the most of the journey from Managua.
What's included in the cleaning fee?
The cleaning fee covers a full clean before your arrival and after check-out. Daily housekeeping is available as an add-on — ask Nemer.
