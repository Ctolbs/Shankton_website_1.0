The area

Playa Gigante, Nicaragua

🏄
Surf
The surf's best in November. Popoyo and Manzanillo are 20 minutes away. Nemer knows which break fits your level.
🚤
Getting here
20-min walk to Gigante village on shaded jungle trails. 35 min by boat from San Juan del Sur. Danny handles airport pickups from Managua.
🍽
Food
Private chef on request — especially worthwhile for Tower's outdoor dining. Gigante village has local spots. Kitchen stocked for self-catering.

On the bunk room: The top-floor bunk room has ceiling fans but no A/C. It's designed for kids — cooler at night with the breeze. The four main bedrooms are fully air-conditioned. Nicaragua has occasional power outages; Tower has a generator and backup connectivity. We'd rather tell you upfront.
