The area

Belmont Shore, Long Beach, CA

The beaches
Alamitos Bay beach is literally 50m from the front door — calm water, great for families. Walk 2 minutes the other way to the Pacific. Two very different beach experiences, same building.
🍜
2nd Street
Belmont Shore's main strip is a mile walk from the door. Dozens of restaurants, coffee, and bars. Walkable on a summer evening. Jasmin has a short list of her favorites.

Getting here
LGB airport is 10 min away. LAX is 30–45 min depending on traffic. 2 parking spaces included — one per unit. Street parking available on most blocks.
