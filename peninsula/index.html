Common questions

What's the cancellation policy?
Full refund for cancellations made at least 14 days before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations 7–14 days before check-in. No refund within 7 days of check-in. All cancellations must be made in writing to contact@shankton.com.
What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is at 3:00 PM. Check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available depending on adjacent bookings — reach out to Jasmin in advance.
Where do I park?
Each unit has 2 dedicated off-street parking spaces included. No street parking required. Check-in instructions will include the specific spaces for your unit.
How far is the beach?
50 meters to Alamitos Bay beach. The Pacific beach at the tip of the Peninsula is a 10-minute walk. Beach chairs and umbrella are provided in each unit.
Can I book both units together?
Yes — whole-building bookings sleep 12 across both units with two full kitchens, two rooftop decks, and two entrances. Email Jasmin at contact@shankton.com for whole-building availability and pricing.
Can I bring a pet?
Pets are not permitted at Shankton Peninsula.
Is there a minimum stay?
Minimum stay is 1 night. A 2-night minimum applies on some holiday weekends.
What's included in the cleaning fee?
The cleaning fee covers a full clean before your arrival and after check-out. Each unit has an in-unit washer and dryer for use during your stay.
What's the Long Beach TOT tax?
Long Beach charges a Transient Occupancy Tax (TOT) of 13.02% on short-term rentals. This is collected at checkout and remitted to the city — it's a government tax, not a Shankton fee.
