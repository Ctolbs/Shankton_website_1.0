Effective June 2026 · Applies to all direct bookings via shankton.com

Cancellation Policy

Cancel at least 14 days before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations made within 14 days of check-in are non-refundable.

To cancel, email contact@shankton.com with your booking reference. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Date changes are subject to availability and must also be requested at least 14 days before the original check-in date. Shankton Properties reserves the right to cancel a booking in exceptional circumstances, in which case a full refund will be issued.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in is from 4:00 PM on your arrival date. Check-out is by 11:00 AM on your departure date. Early check-in or late check-out may be available on request but cannot be guaranteed.

Check-in instructions, door code, and WiFi details will be sent to your email address 48 hours before arrival.

Maximum Occupancy

The number of guests may not exceed the maximum occupancy stated on the property listing. Unauthorized guests are not permitted. Violation may result in immediate termination of the stay without refund.

House Rules

  • Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Please be respectful of neighbors.
  • No smoking inside any property. Designated outdoor areas may be available.
  • Pets are welcome only at properties where a pet fee has been charged. Unauthorized pets may result in additional fees.
  • All properties are privately owned. Treat them with care.

Shankton Properties is not liable for loss of personal property, injury, or any other damages occurring during a guest's stay. Guests assume full responsibility for their own safety and the safety of their party.

Pet Policy

Pets are permitted only at properties that collect a pet fee at the time of booking. Pets must be kept off furniture and must not be left unsupervised in the property. Any damage caused by pets is the guest's responsibility.
