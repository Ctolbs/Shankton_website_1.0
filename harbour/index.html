The area

Playa Gigante, Nicaragua

🏄
Surf
The surf's best in November. Popoyo and Manzanillo are 20 minutes away. Nemer knows which break fits your level.
🚤
Getting here
20-min walk to Gigante village on shaded jungle trails. 35 min by boat from San Juan del Sur. Danny handles airport pickups from Managua.
🍽
Food
We provide salt, pepper, hot sauce, olive oil, cooking oil, and filtered spring water (5-gallon jugs, more on request). If you're self-catering, stop at Colonia in Rivas — about 40 minutes out — on the way in. Gigante's village shops are limited. Private chef available on request.

On power and WiFi: Nicaragua has occasional outages. WiFi is available and we keep connectivity reliable. We'd rather tell you upfront than have it be a surprise.
